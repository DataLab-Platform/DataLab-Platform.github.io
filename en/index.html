DataLab#

DataLab is an open-source platform for scientific and technical data processing and visualization with unique features designed to meet industrial requirements. Leveraging the richness of the scientific Python ecosystem [1], DataLab serves as a complementary tool for your signal and image processing workflows. It can be extended with your Python code through Plugins or directly from your IDE or your Jupyter notebooks. Go to Installation to get started!
